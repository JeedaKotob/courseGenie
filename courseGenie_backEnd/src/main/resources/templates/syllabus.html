Course Policies:

Classroom conduct

  • The rules of decorum apply in class: be respectful of me and of peers, ask productive questions, make appropriate and helpful observations…
  • Electronic devices—mobiles, tablets, and laptops—must be off and put away during class unless used for a specific activity.
  • No naps, no snacks. If you are too tired or hungry, take the absence.
  • If you work consistently and conscientiously, I will “round up” the “ones” before posting final grades.

Infractions of these rules of conduct will negatively impact your final grade.

Course Management

  • You are responsible for knowing what’s due and when by referring to the syllabus.
  • Coursework is cumulative—complete them conscientiously.
  • Type-written work must be turned in through MyCourses and in hard copy by the fixed deadlines.
  • Individual work must be your own.
  • Late work will be deducted 10% every calendar day without exception.
  • Things may change but such changes will be communicated in writing.
